High Striker

Rehabilitation game for people with Parkinson’s
Tools: Unity, C#
June 2024 – October 2024

  • Players move a weight upward by tapping quickly.
  • Difficulty increases by making the weight heavier and fall faster.
  • After 3 failed attempts, the game adapts by reducing weight.
  • Collected data includes age, gender, medication info, finger speed, and levels passed.
